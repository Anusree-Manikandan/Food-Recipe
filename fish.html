🐟 Fish Fry Recipe 🐟

Servings: 4
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

🛒 Ingredients:

Basic Ingredients:

  • 500g Fish Fillets (Kingfish, Pomfret, or any firm fish)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Rice Flour (for crispiness)
  • 1 tsp Salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp Curd (Yogurt) (optional, for marinade)
  • 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves (finely chopped for garnish)

For Frying:

  • Oil (for shallow frying)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

In a bowl, mix ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, lemon juice, salt, and curd (if using).
Add rice flour to the marinade to make the coating crispy. Mix well.

Step 2: Marinate the Fish

Coat the fish fillets evenly with the marinade, ensuring every piece is well coated.
Let the fish marinate for at least 15-20 minutes (or up to 1 hour if you have time).

Step 3: Fry the Fish

Heat oil in a shallow frying pan on medium heat.
Once the oil is hot, carefully add the marinated fish fillets. Fry in batches if needed.
Cook the fish for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy. Flip gently to avoid breaking the fillets.
Once cooked, remove the fish from the pan and drain excess oil on a paper towel.

Step 4: Garnish and Serve

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 5: Serve

Serve the fish fry hot with rice, sambar, or as a starter with onion rings and lemon wedges.

⭐ Tips for Perfect Fish Fry

✅ Choose firm fish like Kingfish, Pomfret, or Seer fish for better texture.
✅ Rice flour is key for a crispy coating, but you can substitute it with cornflour for a lighter crisp.
✅ For extra flavor, you can add a pinch of chat masala or garam masala to the marinade.
✅ If you prefer a tangier taste, squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the fish after frying.
✅ Fry the fish on medium heat to avoid burning the outer layer before the fish is fully cooked inside.

Enjoy your crispy and flavorful Fish Fry! 🐟😋

🐟 Fish Kulambu (Fish Curry) 🍲

Servings: 4
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 30-40 minutes

🛒 Ingredients:

Basic Ingredients:

  • 500g Fish (Kingfish, Seer fish, or any firm fish, cut into pieces)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1-2 Curry Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Pulp (or small lemon-sized tamarind ball)
  • 1 tbsp Salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

For Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp Oil (coconut oil or vegetable oil)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare Tamarind Water

Soak tamarind in warm water for 10-15 minutes and extract the juice, discarding the fibers.

Step 2: Temper the Spices

Heat oil in a large pan or wok.
Add mustard seeds and fennel seeds. Let them splutter for a few seconds.
Add curry leaves and slit green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

Step 3: Sauté the Onions and Tomatoes

Add the chopped onions and sauté until they become soft and golden brown.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
Add the chopped tomatoes (or pureed tomatoes) and cook until they become soft and the oil begins to separate.

Step 4: Add Spices

Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder to the pan. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add the tamarind extract and salt. Stir to combine and let the curry simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 5: Add the Fish

Add the fish pieces to the curry. Stir gently to avoid breaking the fish.
Let the fish cook in the curry for 10-15 minutes until the fish is tender and the flavors have melded together.

Step 6: Simmer and Garnish

Once the fish is cooked, let the curry simmer for an additional 5 minutes to thicken the gravy.
Check for seasoning and adjust salt/spices as needed.
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 7: Serve

Serve the Fish Kulambu with steamed rice or idli.

⭐ Tips for Perfect Fish Kulambu

✅ Use fresh tamarind for a more authentic and tangy flavor.
✅ Adjust the spice level by increasing or reducing the amount of chili powder and green chilies.
✅ For a thicker gravy, you can blend the sautéed onions and tomatoes and then add them back to the curry.
✅ To enhance flavor, coconut milk can be added for a creamy version of the curry.

Enjoy your spicy and flavorful Fish Kulambu! 🍛🐟
