🍋 Lemon Rice Recipe 🍋

Servings: 2-3
Preparation Time: 5 minutes
Cooking Time: 10 minutes

🛒 Ingredients:

Basic Things:

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice (cooled)
  • 1 Lemon (juice extracted)
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp Peanuts or Cashews
  • ½ tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt (adjust to taste)

For Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • 1 tsp Chana Dal
  • 1 sprig Curry Leaves
  • 1 Dry Red Chili
  • 1 pinch Asafoetida (Hing)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
Add urad dal, chana dal, peanuts/cashews, red chili, curry leaves, and green chilies. Fry until golden brown.
Add turmeric powder and salt, mix well. Turn off the heat.
Add lemon juice and mix.
Add the cooked rice, mix gently.
Serve with papad or pickle!

🍅 Tomato Rice Recipe 🍅

Servings: 2-3
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes

🛒 Ingredients:

Basic Things:

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice
  • 2 Tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • ½ tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Leaves (chopped)

For Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • 1 sprig Curry Leaves

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, and green chilies.
Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
Add the cooked rice, mix well, and cook for 2 minutes.
Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with raita or papad.

🥣 Curd Rice Recipe 🥣

Servings: 2-3
Preparation Time: 5 minutes
Cooking Time: 5 minutes

🛒 Ingredients:

Basic Things:

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice (soft and mushy)
  • 1 cup Thick Curd (Yogurt)
  • ½ cup Milk (optional, for a creamy texture)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Grated Carrot (optional)
  • ½ tsp Salt (adjust to taste)

For Tempering:

  • 1 tsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • 1 sprig Curry Leaves
  • 1 Dry Red Chili
  • 1 pinch Asafoetida (Hing)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

Mash the cooked rice and mix with curd, milk, salt, and green chili.
Heat oil, add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, red chili, and hing. Sauté for a few seconds.
Pour the tempering over the curd rice and mix well.
Garnish with coriander leaves and grated carrot.
Serve with pickle or fried mor milagai (sun-dried chili)!

Enjoy your South Indian rice varieties! 🍛😋
