Stir in fresh cream (if using) for a rich texture.
Garnish with coriander leaves.

Step 6: Serve

Serve the chicken gravy with rice, chapati, or paratha.

Basic Things:

  • 500g Boneless Chicken (cut into bite-sized cubes)
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour

  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar

For the Gravy:

  • 1 tbsp Oil

  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Sugar (optional, to balance flavors)
  • 1 cup Water (adjust for gravy consistency)
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, mix boneless chicken with cornflour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, vinegar, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
Let the chicken marinate for 15-20 minutes.

Step 2: Fry the Chicken

Heat oil in a pan or wok.
Fry the marinated chicken pieces in batches until golden and crispy. Remove and set aside on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Step 3: Prepare the Gravy

In the same pan, add a little oil and saut√© sliced onions, chopped garlic, and green chilies until onions become soft and translucent.
Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, sugar (optional), and water. Mix well and bring it to a simmer.
Once it begins to boil, add the cornflour mixture (cornflour mixed with water) to thicken the gravy. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.

Step 4: Combine Chicken and Gravy

Add the fried chicken pieces into the gravy and toss them well until the chicken is coated with the sauce.
Let it cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat for the flavors to blend together.

Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with fried rice, noodles, or as a starter.
